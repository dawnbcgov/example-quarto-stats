The Ministry of Environment and Parks in collaboration with the Northern
Health Authority has ended the Air Quality Warning that was issued on
March 13, 2026 for Smithers due to elevated levels of coarse particulate
matter.

Local air quality has improved due to changing meteorological
conditions. Although the 24-hour concentration remains above the Air
Quality Objective, the hourly concentrations have returned to typical
levels.

## More information
